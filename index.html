Summit Notes is a personal journal of trails walked, peaks reached, and lessons learned one altitude metre at a time. From rocky ridgelines to mist-covered valleys — every path has a story worth telling.

About the Journal

Why we hike

There is something irreplaceable about moving through wild landscape on foot. No engine, no signal, no agenda — just the crunch of gravel underboot and the slow reward of elevation gained. This journal collects those moments: the route notes, the wrong turns, and the views that make every blister entirely worth it.

Whether you are a weekend wanderer or a seasoned thru-hiker, you will find honest trail accounts, practical gear notes, and the occasional recipe for a summit sandwich.
